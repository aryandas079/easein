Privacy First

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February, 2026

"At easein, we believe privacy is the foundation of focus. We don't just protect your data; we minimize its existence."

1. Information We Collect

To help you break down tasks, we collect minimal data: your task titles and your preferred accessibility settings (like Dyslexia Font mode). We do not track your location, your contacts, or any data unrelated to your productivity.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the "atomization" service. This means your tasks stay within your session to help you stay organized. We do not sell your data to advertisers. We don't believe your focus should be for sale.

3. Secure Storage

We use industry-standard encryption to ensure that your task lists remain yours alone. Since we are built for neurodiversity, we understand that "clutter" includes digital risk; we keep our backend as minimal as our frontend.

4. Third-Party Services

We do not share your personal information with third parties unless it is absolutely necessary for the core functionality of the app (such as processing a subscription). In such cases, we only work with partners who meet our strict privacy standards.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use essential cookies solely to remember your theme preferences (Dark Mode) and your font settings. We do not use tracking pixels or intrusive marketing cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, export, or delete your data at any time. Because we value clarity, these options are always easily accessible within your settings menu.
