Accessibility

How we built easein to work for every mind.

Our Mission

We believe that accessibility isn't a "feature"—it's a human right. Traditional productivity tools are often built for neurotypical brains, filled with cluttered sidebars, aggressive notifications, and high-density text. easein is built from the ground up to reduce cognitive friction.

Aa

Lexend Typeface

We use Lexend as our primary font, specifically designed to reduce visual noise and improve reading speed for individuals with dyslexia.

✦

Calming Dark Mode

High-contrast white backgrounds can cause eye strain and sensory overload. Our dark mode uses soft charcoals and deep indigos to soothe the eyes.

◌

Task Atomization

For ADHD users, starting is the hardest part. Our UI focuses on one tiny "atom" of a task at a time, preventing executive function paralysis.

◍

Visual Anchoring

The custom dot cursor serves as a visual anchor, making it easier to track movement across the screen without losing focus.

Continuous Improvement

Accessibility is a journey, not a destination. We are constantly testing easein with neurodiverse communities to ensure we are removing barriers, not adding them. If you find something that feels overwhelming, please tell us.
